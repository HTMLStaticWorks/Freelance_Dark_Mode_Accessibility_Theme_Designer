Our Methodology

1. Contrast Auditing

We analyze existing brand colors and mathematically derive accessible dark mode variants that meet WCAG AAA standards.

2. Token Architecture

Establishing semantic CSS variables to ensure seamless theming across complex React/Vue/Vanilla ecosystems.

3. Simulator Testing

Running the UI through colorblindness simulators (Protanopia, Deuteranopia) to guarantee universal readability.

Frequently Asked Questions

What standards do you audit against?

We strictly follow WCAG 2.1 and WCAG 2.2 guidelines, targeting Level AA as standard and Level AAA for critical typography and contrast elements.

Do you implement the fixes?

Yes! While the Audit Only plan just provides a report, our Theme Architecture plan includes us pushing PRs directly to your codebase to fix CSS and HTML structural issues.

How long does a typical audit take?

For standard SaaS dashboards, an initial audit typically takes 5-7 business days. Complex enterprise platforms may take up to 2 weeks for a comprehensive sweep.

Is there a discount for annual billing?

Yes, we offer a 20% discount on all plans when billed annually. This allows for continuous accessibility monitoring and theme maintenance at a reduced cost.
